Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Canvas-AI is a desktop app that runs on your computer. This policy explains what it does and doesn't do with your data.

What stays on your device

Your Canvas login session, your course content, your settings, and your writing are stored locally on your computer. Canvas-AI does not send your coursework to us.

Who your data is shared with

  • Your Canvas server — the app talks directly to your school's Canvas to read your courses, using your own login.
  • Your chosen AI provider — when you use AI features, the relevant text is sent to the AI you selected (your Claude subscription, a local model that stays on your machine, or the Anthropic API). Their terms and privacy policies apply.
  • Payment & licensing — purchases and license activation are handled by our payment provider (LemonSqueezy). We receive your email and order details to deliver the product and support.

What we collect

If you buy a license, we (via LemonSqueezy) process your email and purchase information to deliver your license and provide support. The app may check your license key and the latest version online.

Your choices

You can use a fully local AI model to keep everything on your machine. You can delete the app and its local data at any time. For data requests, contact us below.
