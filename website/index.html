FAQ

What do I need to run it?

Windows 10/11, a Canvas (Instructure) account, and an AI brain: your Claude Pro/Max subscription, a free local model (Ollama), or an Anthropic API key. The installer can set the AI up for you.

Is my data private?

Yes. Canvas-AI runs on your own machine and your own AI. Your coursework isn't routed through us. See our Privacy Policy.

Does it work with New Quizzes?

It reads classic course content and helps you study. Some Canvas features vary by school.

Can I use it on more than one computer?

Each license activates on one device. Need another? Contact support.

How do refunds work?

See our Refund Policy — reach out and we'll make it right.

Is this allowed by my school?

Canvas-AI is built to help you learn and understand your coursework. Always follow your school's academic-integrity policy.
